Final Project Content

For my final project, I creaed a remake of the popular Atari game Tetris in Scratch. I used custom blocks to check the lines, create a gravity mechanic, and to cancel the falling. I used variables to keep track of the score and make the movements more smooth. I used lists to change the background colors during the game as well as a list to change the block. A list was necessary to make the next block random. With the arrow keys, you could speed up the block, rotate it, and move it left and right. I used all of my knowledge I accumulated over this semester to create a game that uses all of the concepts covered.
